For Designers

We utilize the latest BIM software, including Revit, Auto CAD, Navis Works, Recap, and Blender.

We can produce BIM models with LODs ranging from 100 to 500. (Level 2 LOD 1 to LOD 7). Our BIM models facilitate interoperability and allow for greater coordination flexibility and customizations.





Design Stage

We can produce design stage BIM models from LOD 100 to LOD 300 (LOD1 to LOD4) with accompanying 2D Design Layouts



Walkthroughs

We can create video clips and Virtual Reality walkthroughs of your model to demonstrate to your client or design team.

We can produce As-Built LOD500 (LOD7) BIM models for handover documentation.



Clash Detection

To detect clashes between services and other building aspects, we can use a variety of clash detection algorithms.
To minimise costly delays, we can work with you to address these conflicts before building begins in the affected areas.
