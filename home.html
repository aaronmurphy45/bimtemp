BIMCO offers Building Information Modelling (BIM) solutions to Architecture, Engineering & Construction (AEC) firms worldwide. Clients use BIM services for construction projects to facilitate project coordination, collaboration, asset management, risk mitigation, logistic planning and cost optimization.

BIM implementation helps our clients to assemble projects in a virtual environment for identifying and correcting potential problems before construction.

BIMCO believes “Construction is all about Preconstruction”.

We provide BIM solutions to national and international market. Our team has successfully executed BIM projects across industries include Airports, Hospitals, Medical Centres, Stadium, High Rise Towers, Infrastructure, University Campuses and more. Our process orientation & quality control is as per ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 standards and ISO19650 Organization and digitization of information about buildings and civil engineering works, including building information modelling (BIM).
